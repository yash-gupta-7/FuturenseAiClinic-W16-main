# Graduate Admission Policy

**Document owner:** Graduate School
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Minimum Requirements
Applicants to graduate programs must hold a bachelor's degree from a regionally accredited institution (or international equivalent) and have a minimum **undergraduate cumulative GPA of 3.0** on a 4.0 scale. Individual programs may set higher thresholds.

## 2. Standardized Tests
The **GRE is optional for most master's programs**. The following programs **require** the GRE or a program-specific exam:

- Master's and doctoral programs in Psychology and in the Biological Sciences (GRE required).
- The Master of Business Administration (GMAT or GRE required; waivers available for applicants with 5+ years of managerial experience).

## 3. Required Application Materials
- Statement of Purpose (SOP).
- **Three letters of recommendation** (at least two academic for thesis-track programs).
- Official transcripts from all institutions attended.
- Current résumé or CV.

## 4. English Proficiency
International graduate applicants must meet the graduate English proficiency thresholds in the International Admission Policy (TOEFL iBT 90 / IELTS 6.5).

## 5. Deadlines
Most programs admit for fall only, with a **January 15 priority deadline** for funding consideration (assistantships and fellowships). Programs with rolling admission are listed on each department's page.
