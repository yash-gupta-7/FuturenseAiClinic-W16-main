# Academic Calendar and Registration Policy

**Document owner:** Office of the Registrar
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Term Structure
The University operates on a **two-semester (fall and spring) calendar**, each 15 weeks of instruction plus a final examination week, with an optional summer session of two six-week terms.

## 2. Key Fall 2025 Dates
- Classes begin: **August 25, 2025**
- **Add/drop deadline (last day to add or drop without a W): end of Week 2 (September 5, 2025)**
- Last day to withdraw with a "W" grade: **end of Week 10 (November 7, 2025)**
- Thanksgiving recess: November 26–28, 2025
- Final examinations: December 8–12, 2025

## 3. Add/Drop vs. Withdrawal
- **Dropping** a course during the add/drop period removes it from the record with no transcript notation and follows the per-course refund schedule.
- **Withdrawing** after add/drop results in a "W" on the transcript. A "W" does not affect GPA but counts as attempted credits for Satisfactory Academic Progress.

## 4. Full-Time Status
Undergraduates must enroll in at least **12 credits** to be considered full time. Graduate full-time status is **9 credits**.

## 5. Registration Holds
Outstanding balances, missing immunization records, or advising holds prevent registration until cleared.
