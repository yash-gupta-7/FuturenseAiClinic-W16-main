# Application Fee Waiver Policy

**Document owner:** Office of Admissions
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Purpose
The University offers **application fee waivers** so that the standard application fee is not a barrier for low-income domestic applicants.

## 2. Eligibility (Domestic Applicants)
A domestic applicant qualifies for an application fee waiver if **any** of the following applies:

- The applicant qualifies for the **federal free or reduced-price lunch** program.
- The applicant is **Pell Grant eligible**, or family income falls within USDA income-eligibility limits.
- The applicant received an **SAT or ACT testing fee waiver**.
- A school counselor, principal, or authorized community organization submits a written fee-waiver request on the applicant's behalf.

## 3. What the Waiver Covers
The fee waiver covers **only the application fee**. It does **not** cover the enrollment deposit, housing deposit, transcript fees, or the credential-evaluation fee for international applicants.

## 4. How to Request
Applicants select the fee-waiver option in the application and indicate the qualifying basis. The Office of Admissions may request brief supporting documentation but will not delay review while documentation is pending.

## 5. International Applicants
Application fee waivers are limited to domestic applicants. International applicants experiencing financial hardship should contact the Office of International Admissions to discuss options.
