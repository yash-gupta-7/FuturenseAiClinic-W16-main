# Housing and Residence Life Policy

**Document owner:** Office of Residence Life
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. First-Year Residency Requirement
All first-year (first-time, full-time) undergraduate students are **required to live in University housing** for their first two semesters. This requirement supports academic success and community integration.

## 2. Exemptions
A first-year student may be exempted from the residency requirement if they:

- **Commute from a permanent family home within 30 miles** of campus;
- Are **21 years of age or older** at the start of the term;
- Are **married or have dependent children**;
- Are a **military veteran**; or
- Have a documented medical or religious need approved by Residence Life.

Exemption requests are submitted through the housing portal before the housing deadline.

## 3. Housing Deposit
A **non-refundable housing deposit of $300** reserves a room assignment. The deposit is credited toward the first semester's housing charge. The housing deposit is separate from the enrollment deposit.

## 4. Room Assignments
Assignments are made in the order deposits are received. Students may list roommate preferences, which are honored when mutual and submitted by the deadline.

## 5. Accommodations
Students needing accessibility-related housing accommodations must register with Disability Services; see the Disability Services Policy.
