# Readmission Policy

**Document owner:** Office of the Registrar
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Who Needs Readmission
A former student in good standing who left the University and did not enroll for **two or more consecutive primary semesters (fall/spring)** must apply for readmission before returning.

## 2. Returning Within Two Years
Students returning within two years of their last enrollment, and who left in good academic standing, complete a short readmission application. There is no application fee, and previously earned credits remain valid.

## 3. Returning After Two Years
Students absent for **more than two years must submit a new full application** for admission. Previously earned University credits are re-evaluated against current degree requirements; credits older than ten years may require department review for currency.

## 4. Readmission After Academic Dismissal
A student dismissed for academic reasons may petition for readmission only after **one full semester away** and must submit a written plan describing the changes that will support success. Readmission after dismissal is not guaranteed and may carry conditions (for example, a reduced course load or required advising).

## 5. Financial Holds
Readmission is not granted while an unpaid balance remains on the student account. Outstanding balances must be cleared with the Bursar before the readmission application is reviewed.

## 6. Catalog Year
Readmitted students follow the degree requirements of the catalog in effect at the time of readmission, unless a department approves continuation under the original catalog.
