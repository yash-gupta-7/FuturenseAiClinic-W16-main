# Immunization and Student Health Policy

**Document owner:** Student Health Services
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Required Immunizations
Before enrolling, all new students must submit proof of the following immunizations:

- **Measles, Mumps, and Rubella (MMR):** two doses.
- **Meningococcal conjugate (MenACWY):** one dose on or after the 16th birthday for students under 22.
- **Tetanus-diphtheria-pertussis (Tdap):** within the last 10 years.

A tuberculosis (TB) risk screening is required for international students and others with risk factors.

## 2. Deadline and Holds
Immunization records are due **before the first day of classes**. Students with incomplete records receive a **health hold that blocks registration** for the following term until resolved.

## 3. Exemptions
- **Medical exemption:** requires a signed statement from a licensed provider.
- **Religious exemption:** requires a signed statement of sincerely held belief.

Exempt students may be excluded from campus during a declared outbreak of a vaccine-preventable disease.

## 4. Student Health Insurance
All full-time students must carry health insurance. Students are automatically enrolled in the University-sponsored plan unless they submit proof of comparable coverage and waive the plan by the published deadline.

## 5. Confidentiality
Health and immunization records are protected and maintained separately from academic records.
