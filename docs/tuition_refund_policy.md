# Tuition Refund Policy

**Document owner:** Office of the Bursar
**Last updated:** Fall 2025

## 1. Scope
This policy governs refunds of tuition when a student withdraws from **all** courses in a term. Reducing course load without fully withdrawing is governed by the per-course drop schedule in the Academic Calendar.

## 2. Refund Schedule (Full Withdrawal)
Refunds are calculated from the **first day of the semester**, based on the week in which the official withdrawal is processed:

| Withdrawal processed during | Tuition refund |
| :--- | :---: |
| Week 1 | 100% |
| Week 2 | 75% |
| Week 3 | 50% |
| Week 4 | 25% |
| Week 5 and after | 0% |

## 3. Fees
Mandatory fees (technology, activity, health) and the enrollment deposit are **non-refundable** regardless of withdrawal date.

## 4. Financial Aid Recipients
For students who received federal aid, the federal Return of Title IV Funds calculation may require repayment of a portion of disbursed aid. This calculation is separate from, and may reduce, the institutional refund above.

## 5. Medical and Administrative Withdrawals
A documented medical withdrawal may qualify for a prorated refund beyond the standard schedule, subject to review by the Bursar and the Dean of Students.
